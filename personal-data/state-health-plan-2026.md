### Notes on Rates
1. Salary-based rates apply to subscriber-only tier, based on total base pay.
2. Subscriber-only rate (set at Open Enrollment) stays the same for 2026 regardless of salary adjustments.
3. Contracts less than 12 months — contact your Health Benefits Representative for rates.
4. If you or a dependent are Medicare eligible (active employment), State Health Plan is primary insurer.
5. Local government employees — contact your Health Benefits Representative for rates.
6. **Employer share for Active subscribers: $742.04/month.**
