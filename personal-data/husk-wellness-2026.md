# 🥗 HUSK Nutrition & Wellness

*Source: `assets/documents/CCCC_-_HUSK_Nutrition_Flyer.pdf`*

CCCC employees, spouses, and dependents can meet with a **HUSK Registered Dietitian** for a customized one-on-one nutrition consultation — covered by insurance.

## What's Offered

- Recipes
- Mindful eating
- Managing stress
- Developing healthful habits
- Disease management
- And more

## Coverage

- Up to **6 visits** may be covered by Blue Cross Blue Shield of NC insurance.
- HUSK is in-network with many other insurance carriers — they can verify your coverage.

## App Features (HUSK Nutrition App)

- Goal tracking
- Food tracking
- Direct messaging to your Registered Dietitian
- Appointment booking

> **Bonus:** Download the app and attend 6 visits with an RD to earn a **FREE biometric scale**.
