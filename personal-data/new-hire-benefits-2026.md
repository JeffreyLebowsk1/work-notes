Benefits enrollment information for new CCCC employees. Full plan details at [mymarkiii.com/centralcarolinacc](https://mymarkiii.com/centralcarolinacc/).

## ⚠️ Important — Enrollment Deadline

You have **30 days from your hire date** to enroll in benefits. If you wait until next year (open enrollment), you may have to answer health questions for certain plans.

Enroll your dependents with: full name, date of birth, Social Security number, and any required supporting documents.

## Flexible Spending Account (FSA)

- Pre-tax dollars for healthcare and dependent care expenses
- Saves approximately **$27.65–$37.65 per $100** placed in the plan (depending on federal tax bracket)
- Must enroll by plan start date; deductions begin on first pay period after plan start
- Eligible only for full-time employees working 30+ hours/week on first of month after hire date
- Plan year: January 1 – December 31, 2026

## Life Insurance (The Hartford)

- Basic and supplemental group term life coverage
- New hires may qualify for **Guaranteed Issue** amounts (no health questions)
- Waiting to enroll may require Evidence of Insurability
- Includes grief support, estate administration guidance, and identity theft protection for beneficiaries
