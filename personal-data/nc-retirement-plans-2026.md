## How to Enroll (New Account)

1. Visit [myNCPlans.gov](https://myNCPlans.gov) and select **REGISTER**.
2. Select **"I have a Plan Enrollment Code"** and follow the prompts using the Group Id and Enrollment Code above.
3. The website guides you through the full enrollment process.

## Already Have an Empower Account?

1. You will land on a **"We Found You!"** page.
2. Click **Log In**.
3. Select **REGISTER**.
4. Select **"I do not have a PIN"** and follow prompts to complete registration and enrollment.

> **Note:** If your account is currently inactive, contact your employer to reactivate it before enrolling online.

## Future Logins

- Visit [myNCPlans.gov](https://myNCPlans.gov) and enter your username and password.
- A verification code will be sent for enhanced security; check **"Remember this device"** to skip in the future.

## Key Differences: 401(k) vs. 457

| | NC 401(k) | NC 457 |
|---|---|---|
| Type | Traditional 401(k) | Governmental 457(b) deferred compensation |
| Early withdrawal penalty | 10% penalty before age 59½ (with exceptions) | No 10% early withdrawal penalty |
| Contribution limit (2026) | $23,500 | $23,500 |
| Can contribute to both? | ✅ Yes | ✅ Yes |
