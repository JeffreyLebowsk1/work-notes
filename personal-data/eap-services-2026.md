## Services Available

### Assessment & Counseling
- Available 24/7/365 by phone
- Face-to-face, virtual, or telephonic sessions with a licensed clinician
- BetterHelp available for virtual sessions
- Common issues: stress, relationship/marital issues, parenting, work concerns, depression, alcohol/drug use, grief, and more

### Legal Services
- Free telephonic legal advice
- Free 30-minute appointment with a local attorney
- 25% discount on ongoing legal services (in most cases)
- Downloadable legal forms (wills, death certificate requests, etc.)
- Online legal encyclopedia
- Does **not** cover disputes involving the employer, EAP, or business issues

### Financial Services
- Free financial counseling appointments
- Topics: bankruptcy, budgeting, home buying, college savings, retirement planning
- 40 financial calculators online
- ID theft recovery through credit monitoring
- Discounted credit reports

### Online Services
- 7 content divisions: Parenting, Aging, Balancing, Thriving, Living, Working, International
- Monthly online seminars and eLearning courses with certificates
- Searchable databases: childcare, elder care, adoption, attorneys, financial planners, pet sitting, schools/colleges, volunteer opportunities
- 100+ streaming audio and video files on health topics
- Savings Center: up to 25% discounts on name-brand items
- Relocation Center: community preview tool
