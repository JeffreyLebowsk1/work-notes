# FERPA Quick Reference

## Key Rights Under FERPA
- Students have the right to inspect and review their education records
- Students have the right to request amendment of inaccurate records
- Students have the right to consent to disclosure of personally identifiable information
- Students have the right to file a complaint with the U.S. Department of Education
