# Graduation Petitions

## Petition Types

| Type | Description |
|---|---|
| **Late Graduation Application** | Student missed the application deadline but wishes to graduate the current term |
| **Course Substitution** | Student requests that a different course be accepted in place of a required course |
| **Waiver of Requirement** | Student requests that a specific requirement be waived (rare; requires strong justification) |
| **Transfer Credit Exception** | Student requests that a transfer course be counted toward a specific requirement |
| **Graduation with In-Progress Course** | Student expects to complete a requirement by end of term — conditional graduation |
| **Change of Graduation Term** | Student wishes to move graduation to a different term (earlier or later) |
| **Degree Program Change** | Student requests a change to their degree or major on the graduation application |

---

## Petition Process

1. **Student submits petition form** to the Registrar's Office (in person, via portal, or email).
   - Petition should include: student ID, degree program, petition type, explanation/justification, and any supporting documentation.
2. **Initial review** by Registrar staff:
   - Confirm petition is complete.
   - Verify student's enrollment and application status in SIS.
3. **Route to appropriate decision-maker** based on petition type:
   - Academic petitions (substitutions, waivers) → **Academic Advisor + Department Chair** (and sometimes Dean)
   - Administrative petitions (late applications, term changes) → **Registrar's Office** (may require supervisor approval)
4. **Decision issued** and communicated to student in writing.
5. **If approved:** Update SIS, degree audit, and graduation list accordingly.
6. **If denied:** Notify student of denial and their options (e.g., appeal, re-apply for a future term).
7. **Log the petition** in the petition log below.

## Common Petition Decisions & Guidance

| Petition Type | Common Outcome | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Late application (missed deadline by < 2 weeks) | Often approved if graduation requirements are met | Confirm space in ceremony program |
| Late application (missed deadline by > 2 weeks) | Moved to next term's ceremony, unless exception granted | Diploma may still be awarded current term |
| Course substitution (very similar course) | Often approved with advisor/department support | Requires dept. chair or curriculum committee approval |
| Course substitution (unrelated course) | Often denied | Students should explore other options |
| Graduation with in-progress course | Conditionally approved with transcript hold | Follow up after final grades post |
| Change of graduation term (moving earlier) | Depends on audit completion | Confirm all requirements can be met |
| Change of graduation term (deferral) | Usually approved | Update candidate list accordingly |
