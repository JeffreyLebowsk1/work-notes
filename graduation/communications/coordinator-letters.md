# Graduation Coordinator Letter Templates

External letters issued by the Graduation Coordinator for individual students.

## Permission to Take Courses at Another Institution

Use this letter when a currently enrolled CCCC student needs institutional permission to take required courses at another college or university for transfer credit.

---

[Date]

[Address Line 1]  
[Address Line 2]

Re: [Student Full Name]

Dear Registrar,

[Student Full Name] is a student in our [Program Name] ([Program Code]) program at Central Carolina Community College in Sanford, NC. [Mr./Ms./Mx.] [Student Last Name] has our permission to complete the following required courses at your institution:

**Course 1**  
Course name: [Course Number and Name]  
Course description: [Description]  
Credit hours: [#] credits

**Course 2** *(if applicable)*  
Course name: [Course Number and Name]  
Course description: [Description]  
Credit hours: [#] credits

It will be the student's responsibility to attain a C- average or better in order to consider these courses for transfer credit.

Sincerely,

Matt Dilworth  
Graduation Coordinator  
Central Carolina Community College

## Usage Notes

- Always issue on official CCCC letterhead.
- For the Completion Letter, only include the **last 4 digits** of SSN (as shown). Never write the full SSN — see [`../../personal-data/`](../../personal-data/) for FERPA guidance.
- Match credential terminology to the student's actual award type: use **"degree"** for AAS/AS/AA programs and **"certificate"** for certificate programs (e.g., "Mason's certificate is expected to be conferred…").
- Verify the student's record in SIS before issuing either letter.
- File a copy of each issued letter in the student's folder.
