# Ceremony Day Checklist

## Before Ceremony
- [ ] Arrive early and confirm venue setup
- [ ] Confirm AV and sound system working
- [ ] Distribute name cards to students
- [ ] Brief faculty marshals
- [ ] Confirm accessibility accommodations in place
- [ ] Confirm photographer/videographer on site

## During Ceremony
- [ ] Student line-up organized
- [ ] Name reader briefed and ready
- [ ] Coordinate student procession
- [ ] Handle any last-minute issues

## After Ceremony
- [ ] Collect any uncollected materials
- [ ] Thank volunteers and staff
- [ ] Document any issues for future reference
- [ ] Confirm photos/video captured
