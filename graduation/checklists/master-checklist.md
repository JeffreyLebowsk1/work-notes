# Master Graduation Checklist

## 4–5 Months Before Ceremony
- [ ] Open graduation applications
- [ ] Communicate deadlines to students and advisors
- [ ] Confirm ceremony date and venue
- [ ] Begin cap & gown vendor coordination

## 3 Months Before Ceremony
- [ ] Close graduation applications
- [ ] Begin degree audit review
- [ ] Compile preliminary graduation candidate list
- [ ] Confirm cap & gown orders

## 2 Months Before Ceremony
- [ ] Complete degree audits
- [ ] Resolve outstanding degree requirements with advisors
- [ ] Finalize graduation candidate list
- [ ] Begin commencement program draft

## 1 Month Before Ceremony
- [ ] Submit commencement program for printing
- [ ] Send ceremony details to graduates
- [ ] Confirm faculty marshals and platform party
- [ ] Arrange accessibility accommodations
- [ ] Confirm AV, photography, and logistics

## 1 Week Before Ceremony
- [ ] Confirm final headcount
- [ ] Finalize student line-up order
- [ ] Conduct rehearsal
- [ ] Prepare name cards / reader list
