## Logistics Checklist

### 3+ Months Out
- [ ] Venue booked and contract signed
- [ ] Date confirmed on institutional academic calendar
- [ ] Cap & gown vendor confirmed and order portal opened for students
- [ ] Faculty marshal coordinator notified

### 1–2 Months Out
- [ ] Faculty marshals confirmed and briefed
- [ ] Photography/videography contract signed
- [ ] AV/sound vendor confirmed
- [ ] Accessibility accommodations arranged (interpreter, accessible seating, etc.)
- [ ] Program draft prepared (verify spelling of all names!)
- [ ] Program submitted for printing

### 2 Weeks Out
- [ ] Final headcount confirmed
- [ ] Student line-up order finalized (alphabetical, by program, or by college — verify format)
- [ ] Name reader list prepared and proofed
- [ ] Rehearsal scheduled and communicated to participants
- [ ] Venue logistics confirmed (parking, doors, AV setup time)

### Week of Ceremony
- [ ] Name cards / reader list distributed
- [ ] Staff roles and positions assigned
- [ ] Rehearsal conducted
- [ ] Emergency contacts and venue staff confirmed

### Day of Ceremony
- [ ] See [Ceremony Day Checklist](../checklists/ceremony-day.md)

## Accessibility & Accommodations

- Reserve clearly marked accessible seating at the front for guests with mobility needs.
- Provide an ASL interpreter if requested or required.
- Confirm accessible routes from parking to the venue.
- Ensure graduates with mobility needs have a modified processional plan.
- Communicate accommodation options to graduates in the ceremony details email.
