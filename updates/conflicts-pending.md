## CONFLICT 1 — High School Transcript Credential Hold

**Affects:** Graduation, Student Records, Financial Aid
**Status:** ⚠️ Awaiting official clarification

### The Conflict
| Source | Position |
|--------|----------|
| **Administrative Decision** | We will **no longer hold credentials** until high school transcripts are received — except for Early College graduates. |
| **Published Catalog** | High school transcripts are **mandatory** for certain applicants (e.g., home-schooled students). Students who have not submitted one may be unable to have financial aid awarded. |

### Why This Matters
This conflict directly impacts **degree issuance** and **financial aid eligibility**. Acting on the administrative decision without catalog alignment could result in releasing credentials to students with missing required documentation, or in students losing financial aid.

International applicants to Dental Hygiene and HIT may be improperly accepted or rejected depending on which policy is followed. The catalog pages may need to be updated if the administrative decision is final.
