# 🏫 College Operating Status Messaging & Guidance

Templates and approved messaging for communicating CCCC's operating status to students, staff, and the public.

---

## Open

> Campuses and sites are operating according to normal schedules.

>

>

## Early Closing

> All College locations will close at **[X:XX] p.m.** on **[DAY], [DATE]**.
>
> All in-person activities after this time are canceled.
>
> Students and instructors should attend the class that is in session until the time campus closes.
>

>
> For additional information, please visit **[URL]**.

## Remote Instruction & Operations

> All online instruction continues as scheduled.
>
> Remote-capable employees are expected to work.
>
> Employees normally required to report on campus should consult the **Flexible Work Arrangements (3.4.7)** policy and coordinate with their supervisor.

---

## Full Closure / Emergency Shutdown

> All College locations are closed on **[DAY], [DATE]**.
>
> In-person classes and activities are canceled.
>
> All online instruction continues as scheduled.
