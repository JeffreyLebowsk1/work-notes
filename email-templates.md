# 📝 Email Templates

## 📄 Transcripts

### Transcript Request Received

Subject: Your Transcript Request Has Been Received

---

Dear [Student Name],

We have received your transcript request. Transcripts are typically processed within [X] business days.

If you have any questions, please contact the Registrar's Office at [contact info].

Registrar's Office

### Transcript Request — Hold on Account

Subject: Action Required — Hold on Your Account

---

For assistance, please contact [relevant office] at [contact info].

[Your Name]
Registrar's Office
