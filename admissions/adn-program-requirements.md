# 🏥 ADN Program Requirements — Humanities/Fine Arts Elective

> **Program:** Associate Degree Nursing (ADN) — **A45110**
> **Applies To:** Registrar's Office staff advising or auditing ADN students

---

## Humanities/Fine Arts Elective — 5th Semester Pick List

The ADN curriculum uses a **specific approved pick list** for the Humanities/Fine Arts elective requirement in the **5th semester**. This list is narrower than the broader Humanities/Fine Arts elective options available for other Associate in Applied Science (AAS) degrees.

Students must choose **one** of the following:

| Course | Title |
|---|---|
| **ART 111** | Art Appreciation |
| **ART 114** | Art History Survey I |
| **ART 115** | Art History Survey II |
| **HUM 115** | Critical Thinking |
| **MUS 110** | Music Appreciation |
| **MUS 112** | Introduction to Jazz |
| **PHI 240** | Introduction to Ethics |

---

## Religion Exception

Although **REL 110**, **REL 211**, and **REL 212** may count as Humanities electives for other majors, they **will not** fulfill the Humanities/Fine Arts elective requirement for the ADN program.

Remind students of this distinction when advising on course selection or reviewing their degree audit.

---

## Grade Requirements

| Coursework Type | Minimum Grade |
|---|---|
| All general education courses (including Humanities) | **C or better** |
| All NUR prefix courses | **B (78 or higher)** |

---

## Degree Audit (EVAL) Guidance

When performing a degree audit (**EVAL**) for an ADN student:

- If a student has completed a Humanities course that is **not on the pick list above** (e.g., a Religion course), the system will likely pull it into **"Other Required Hours"** or leave it as an unused credit.
- **Do not** "force" a Religion course (or any other non-listed Humanities course) into the Humanities slot for ADN without:
  1. **Written approval** from the Nursing Department Chair: **Dr. Barbara Campbell** (Advisor #466734)
  2. A formal **Course Substitution Form** on file with the Student Records and Registrar's Office
