# Admissions Notes

## General Notes

- The Registrar's Office supports the admissions process by maintaining student records, verifying enrollment eligibility, and coordinating data with the Admissions Office.
- The Registrar **does not** make admissions decisions — those are made by the Admissions Office and/or academic departments.
- Key responsibilities: receiving admitted student files, creating new student records in SIS, issuing student IDs, processing transfer credit evaluations.
- Always verify that a student is officially admitted before creating a record or releasing any information.
