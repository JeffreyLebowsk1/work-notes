# Admissions Requirements

> **Note:** These are general frameworks. Always verify current requirements with the Admissions Office and academic departments each term.

## Undergraduate Requirements

### Domestic Applicants
- Completed application form
- Official high school transcript (or GED)
- Official college transcripts (if any prior college coursework)
- SAT/ACT scores (if required — verify current test-optional policy)
- Letters of recommendation (if required by program)
- Personal statement / essay (if required)
- Application fee

### General Requirements

- Letters of recommendation (typically 2–3)

## International Students

In addition to standard requirements:
- Certified English translations of all foreign transcripts
- TOEFL / IELTS / Duolingo English Test scores (if applicable — verify thresholds by program)
- Copy of passport / visa documentation
- Proof of financial support (for visa purposes)
- SEVIS I-20 form issued after admission (coordinate with DSO/International Student Office)

## Important Notes

- Incomplete applications should not be acted upon — return to Admissions for follow-up.
- A student is not officially admitted until a formal admission letter is issued.
- Do not create a student record in SIS until official admission is confirmed.
