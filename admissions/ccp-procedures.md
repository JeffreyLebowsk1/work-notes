## Ending CCP Programs for Graduates

> Confirmed accurate as of 2025-11-06. **Do not NULL them until Jamie gives us a cleanup sheet.**

For high schools whose graduation falls **after our Summer Semester begins**:

### On SPRO

1. End the CCP Program(s) for the **last date of the semester**.
2. Enter **NULL** for Student Type, ending **1 day after** the CCP Program end date.
3. For **Home Location**, end for the **same date** as the CCP Program end.
4. Add Home Location for **LMC, PMC, or HMC** with a start date **1 day after** the CCP Program end date.
5. If not ended automatically when ending the CCP Program(s), end the **CCP Advisor (CHSP)** for the same date as the CCP Program end date.
6. Start a new Advisor for the traditional curriculum program for **Summer Semester on 6/1** of the current year (e.g., 6/1/26).

### On XNC2

1. End pathway for **1 day after** the CCP Program end date.

## CCP Students — General Information

> Reference document: **CCP Student Information 2.11.21 (1).pdf**
> **Do not NULL them until Jamie gives us a cleanup sheet.**

- **C-Step:** Transfer student — can go to UNC Chapel Hill.
- **C-3 Step:** Transfer student — can go to NC State.
- CCCC also has transfer agreements with **Meredith, UNCG, ECU, UNCC, and Wilmington**.
- When a CCP application comes in **after the start of a semester**, the student type must be **backdated**. Students may have an app in progress but are already registered (which is why they appear on error lists).
- If a CCP student **graduates early (December)**, they can take regular college classes. However, if their graduation date from HS/CCP program is **after the start of the next semester (January)**, they can only take classes as a CCP student — not as a traditional college student. Exception: if the student entered the CCP program but then stopped and graduated from HS, they would be considered a traditional college student.
- **Program options:** P1012C-Arts; P1032C-Nursing; P1042C-Science; P1052C-Engineering
- For CCP students transitioning from HS to traditional college student who put in an application: you can enter the new traditional college program, but **leave CCP program as active & primary** — the traditional program will be secondary.
- **⚠️ Chatham Charter** cannot be a CCP student in the year they graduate because they graduate **before our Summer Semester starts**.
- An underage hold can be ended at the request of Nick Testa (or equivalent advisor).
- To see the **drop date** for a CCP student: go to **SRGD**, enter the full class and section, pick a semester, and look at **"Drop End Date"** for the last date to drop.
- To see the **last day to withdraw without WF**: check the **Master Calendar by Locations** Google doc from Jamie.
- Student requests to **transfer from one section to another** are only considered if:
  - Both courses have the same time frame (e.g., 8-week to 8-week).
  - Sections preferably have the same instructor.
  - The request is made **after the Census date**.
  - The reason must make sense — reason cannot simply be to get a different instructor.
