# Attendance Roster Audit — Email Templates

Use these templates when contacting faculty about errors found during attendance roster audits.

---

## Template 1: No Attendance Recorded

**Subject:** Time Sensitive---Please Correct Immediately

---

Greetings,

We are reviewing attendance rosters. Please correct the following error(s):

**Course:** {Course Name/Number}

**Error:** No attendance recorded for the following student(s): {Student Name(s)}

**Instructions:**

- **Online rosters:** Enter a **P-present** status on the date the student submitted their first assignment. For each subsequent assignment due date, mark **P-present** or **A-absent**.
- **Face-to-face / Zoom rosters:** Enter a **P-present** status on the first day the student came to class. For each subsequent meeting, mark **P-present**, **A-absent**, or **L-late**.
- Flag any student who did not attend by the census date as a **No Show**.
- Enter an **LDA** or **No Show** flag for any student who dropped. (Remember to block No Shows and drop students in Blackboard!)
- To add a No Show flag *after* attendance certification: go to the **Faculty** section of Self Service → select the course → **Grading** tab → **Final Grade** tab → scroll to the student → check the **No Show** box next to their name.

**Subject:** Time Sensitive---Please Correct Immediately

---

## Usage Notes

- Replace all `{…}` placeholders with the appropriate values before sending.
- **Template 1** is used when a faculty member has not recorded any attendance for one or more students.
- **Template 2** is used when a faculty member has entered an "E-Excused Absence" code, which is not an allowed attendance status.
- Allowed attendance statuses are: **P** (Present), **A** (Absent), **L** (Late), **LDA** (Last Date of Attendance), and **No Show**.
- Send to the instructor of record for the course. CC the department chair if the issue is not corrected after a follow-up.
- See [`../../personal-data/`](../../personal-data/) for FERPA guidance before including student names in external communications.
