# Never Attended Letter Template

Use this template when responding to inquiries about a student who has **no enrollment record** at Central Carolina Community College.

---

**Subject:** Never Attended Letter

---

To Whom It May Concern:

{Student Name} (DOB {D/O/B}) has never been enrolled at Central Carolina Community College in Sanford, North Carolina. A curriculum transcript is not available for this student, as no courses were completed. Please adjust your records to reflect this information.

If you have any questions, you may contact our office at 919-718-7201 or admissions@cccc.edu.

---

## Usage Notes

- Replace `{Student Name}` with the student's full legal name.
- Replace `{D/O/B}` with the student's date of birth (format: MM/DD/YYYY).
- Verify the inquiry and confirm there is truly no enrollment record in SIS before sending.
- Send via email or on official CCCC letterhead as appropriate.
- Do **not** include SSN or additional PII beyond name and date of birth — see [`../../personal-data/`](../../personal-data/) for FERPA guidance.
