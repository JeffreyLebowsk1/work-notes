# Withdrawal Instructions — Email Template

Use this template when a withdrawal form has been submitted incorrectly or when an instructor needs guidance on the proper withdrawal process.

---

## Template: Withdrawal Form Not Processed — Instructor Guidance

**Subject:** Withdrawal Instructions

---

Good afternoon,

This form has not been processed.

Instructors may only initiate a withdrawal due to non-attendance. Per college policy, an instructor should initiate a withdrawal when a student has exceeded 20% of class time in absences (for fully online classes, this translates to 20% of assignments missed) or when a student has gone two weeks without attending or making contact (2 weeks no show/no contact). To initiate a withdrawal in either of those instances, please use the withdrawal form available on the intranet. When providing the student's LDA, remember it is the last day an assignment was submitted (online meetings) or the last day the student attended F2F or in a Collaborate session.

Here at CCCC, student-initiated withdrawals are handled by the Educational Navigators (counselors) in our Student Onboarding and Success Office (formerly Admissions). Students may reach out to an Ed Nav in person on any of our three main campuses or virtually.
https://www.cccc.edu/onboarding/contacts/

If the student made the request electronically, you can forward the email to an Ed Navigator or direct the student to reach out to that office.

---

## Usage Notes

- Use this template when an instructor submits a withdrawal form for a reason that is **not** non-attendance (e.g., the student requested it).
- **Instructor-initiated withdrawals** are only appropriate when:
  - The student has exceeded **20% of class time in absences** (or 20% of assignments missed for fully online courses), **or**
  - The student has gone **two weeks without attending or making contact** (2 weeks no show/no contact).
- **Student-initiated withdrawals** must go through the Educational Navigators in the Student Onboarding and Success Office — not through the registrar or instructor.
- If the student requested withdrawal via email, the instructor can forward that email directly to an Ed Navigator.
- Ed Nav contacts: https://www.cccc.edu/onboarding/contacts/
