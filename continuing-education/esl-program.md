# ESL Program — English as a Second Language

Reference notes for the English as a Second Language (ESL) program at CCCC, offered through the College & Career Readiness (CCR) division.

> For ABE, Adult High School, and HiSET preparation registration details, see [ABE & CCR Registration](./abe-registration.md). Minor requirements and walk-in process details are the same across all CCR programs.

---

## Eligibility & Cost

- **Age:** Students must be at least **16 years old**.
- **Out-of-School Requirement:** Must have been out of school for a minimum of **six months**.
- **Cost:** Classes are offered **free of charge** to eligible students.

---

## Program Offerings

| Course | Description |
|---|---|
| **English Literacy** | Core communication skills — speaking, reading, and writing. |
| **U.S. Citizenship Preparation** | Assistance with preparing for the naturalization test. |
| **Computer Literacy** | Digital skills training designed for ESL students. |
| **Virtual Classes** | Online ESL options offered via Zoom. |

---

## Registration Guidance

- Registration is **ongoing** and handled through **in-person walk-in sessions**.
- Sessions take **2 to 3 hours** — students must arrive **at the exact start time** to be admitted.
- **Minor students (ages 16–17):** Must complete the **Minor Release Information Packet** before attending any walk-in session. See [ABE & CCR Registration — Minor Requirements](./abe-registration.md#minor-requirements-ages-1617) for staff guidance on handling minors.
