# Personal Enrichment Courses

Reference notes for personal enrichment (Community Service) non-credit courses offered through the Workforce Development Division at CCCC.

---

## Key Talking Points for Students

- **Course Scope:** Classes focus on personal growth and hobbies — popular examples include **digital photography** and **cake decorating**.
- **Credit Status:** These are non-curriculum courses and **do not count** toward a college degree or diploma.
- **Recognition:** Students receive a **certificate of completion** and earn **Continuing Education Units (CEUs)**. One CEU = 10 hours of organized participation.
- **Availability:** Classes are offered day or evening across Chatham, Harnett, and Lee counties.
- **Registration:** Ongoing. Students should check the [CE course schedule](https://www.cccc.edu/ecd/schedule/) for current listings, as offerings vary by semester.

Thank you for your interest in the personal enrichment courses at Central Carolina Community College. These non-credit classes are a wonderful way to explore new hobbies or update your skills in a relaxed environment.

**Key Course Information:**
- **Offerings:** We offer a variety of classes, including popular options like digital photography and cake decorating.

Registration is ongoing. If you have previously completed an enrichment course and need a transcript, you may request one free of charge by completing the **Personal Enrichment & Short-Term Training Transcript Request** form available on our website.
