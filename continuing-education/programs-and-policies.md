## Policy & Registration

- **Credit Type:** Continuing Education Units (CEUs) are awarded — not degree-level credit. These courses generally **do not count** toward a curriculum degree or diploma. One CEU = 10 hours of participation.
- **External Transfers:** CCCC **does not transfer** continuing education credits from other institutions.
- **Internal Articulation:** Students may be granted curriculum credit for specific CCCC non-credit courses if an approved internal articulation agreement is on file in the Registrar's Office.
- **Registration:** Ongoing; available at the Siler City Center, Harnett Main Campus, and Lee Main Campus.
- **Attendance & Refunds:** A 75% refund is available if a student officially withdraws on or before the 10% point of the session. Students may be dropped for two consecutive weeks of unexcused absences.

Non-credit/Continuing Education transcript responsibilities are split between the Workforce Development Division and this office by date.

| Record Type | Who Handles It |
|---|---|
| **Post-2007 CE records** | Student Records and Registrar's Office — (919) 718-7201 / admissions@cccc.edu |
| **Pre-2007 CE records** | Nutan Varma, Harnett Main Campus (CE Building) — (910) 814-8975 / nvarma@cccc.edu |
| **Personal Enrichment / Short-Term Training** | Requires the **"Personal Enrichment & Short-Term Training Transcript Request"** form; handled by Registrar's Office (post-2007) |
| **GED / HSE records** | CCCC does not maintain these — students must order through **[DiplomaSender](https://www.diplomasender.com)** at (855) 313-5799 |

> ⚠️ **Always confirm the year first.** Ask the student whether their CE courses were before or after 2007 before routing the request.

### Pre-2007 CE Transcript Requests — Staff Guidance

- Direct the student to contact Nutan Varma directly by phone or by emailing the Adult High School (AHS) transcript request form.
- Processing time: up to **five business days**.
- **Cost: free of charge.**
- **AHS backup printing:** In urgent situations, Adult High School transcripts can also be printed by:
  - Chesure Tate — Bell Welcome Center, (919) 718-7716
  - Anita Green — Lee Main Campus, (919) 545-8669

### GED/HSE Records — Staff Guidance

CCCC does not maintain official North Carolina High School Equivalency (GED/HiSET) records. If a student asks for these, remind them:

- Records must be ordered through **[DiplomaSender](https://www.diplomasender.com)** at (855) 313-5799.
- The standard fee is **$20.00 per document**.
- Transcripts must be sent directly to CCCC at **admissions@cccc.edu** to be considered official.

## Financial Aid & Funding

| Funding Source | Details |
|---|---|
| **Workforce Pell** | Beginning **July 1, 2026**, students in select CE programs (150–600 clock hours) may become eligible for Pell funding. Refer students to the Financial Aid Office for the eligible program list. |
