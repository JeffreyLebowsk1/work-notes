# The Continuing Education Scholarship Process

> To reduce financial barriers to education, the **Workforce Access Programs Office**, part of Central Carolina Community College's **Workforce Continuing Education Department**, offers and administers several scholarships and grants to help students enroll in short-term occupational training programs.

## Process Steps

### 1. Review Available Scholarships and Classes

View the [CE Scholarships](https://www.cccc.edu/continuingeducation/workforceaccess/) and available CE classes webpages. These provide more information on funding opportunities and classes scheduled to run.

---

### 2. Complete the Online Scholarship Inquiry

Complete the **Continuing Education Online Scholarship Inquiry**. The Workforce Access Programs team will evaluate responses and email information about any funds for which you may be eligible.

---

### 3. Check Your Email

Check your email (including **spam folders**) for any correspondence, applications, or information. The team will use the email(s) submitted on the Inquiry Form. They cannot respond to spam filter requests.

---

### 4. Fill Out and Sign All Applications and Forms

Fill out and sign all applications and forms. Directions are included on all applications. Contact the Workforce Access Programs team with any questions or concerns.

---

### 5. Return All Required Documents

Return all required documents to **conedscholarships@cccc.edu** within **14 calendar days**.

> ⚠️ If completed documents are not received within 14 calendar days, the student will be marked as no longer requesting funds.
>
> Note: Only **complete applications** are considered. Filling out an application does **not** guarantee an award.

---

### 6. Keep Your Award Letter

Once all completed documentation is received, an award letter will be sent via email. Award letters are **class and section-specific**. Keep a copy of the letter for future reference.

---

### 7. Register for Your Awarded Class

Register for the awarded class if you have not already done so. To register, contact **CE Support**:

- 📞 (919) 718-7500
- 📧 cesupport@cccc.edu

> **Note:** Courses have limited seats and a spot cannot be guaranteed. Students may contact CE Support and register for a course before receiving the award letter, then use the award to reimburse the cost of the course.
>
> ⚠️ If the student does not register for the course in a timely manner, the award will be rescinded.

---

### 8. Attendance Verification and Award Processing

Before the award begins to process, attendance will be verified. Once verification is complete, the **Financial Aid** and **Business Offices** will process the award.

- Funds are applied toward any outstanding debts with CCCC first.
- Any remaining balance is mailed as a check made in the student's name to the address on file with CCCC.

> To verify a student's address, contact the **Student Records Office** at **919-718-7201** or the **Dean of Enrollment**.
