## Eligibility & Cost

- **Age:** Students must be at least **16 years old**.
- **Out-of-School Requirement:** Must have been out of school for a minimum of **six months**.
- **Cost:** Classes are offered **free of charge** to eligible students.

---

## Minor Requirements (Ages 16–17)

> ⚠️ **Mandatory before any walk-in session.** Students who are 16 or 17 years old must complete the **Minor Release Information Packet** before attending a walk-in registration session.

This requirement applies to all CCR programs: Adult Basic Education, Adult High School, High School Equivalency (GED/HiSET) preparation, and ESL.

### Staff Guidance — Minors

When assisting a minor over the phone or at the counter:

1. **Confirm the packet is complete** before directing them to any registration site.
2. If they do **not** yet have the packet, direct them to the [College & Career Readiness registration page](https://www.cccc.edu/support-success/all-offices-services/college-career-readiness/registration) to obtain the necessary forms.
3. Do **not** send a minor directly to a walk-in session without first confirming this step.

---

## Registration Process

- Registration is **ongoing** and handled through **in-person walk-in sessions** at Chatham, Harnett, and Lee campuses.
- Sessions take **2 to 3 hours** — students must arrive **at the exact start time** to be admitted.
- No appointments; walk-in only.
