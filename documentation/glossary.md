# 🗂️ Glossary

Definitions of terms, acronyms, status codes, and jargon used in the Registrar's Office and student information system.

---

## A

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| AACRAO | American Association of Collegiate Registrars and Admissions Officers |
| Academic Hold | A restriction placed on a student account preventing registration or transcript release |
| Add/Drop Period | The period at the start of each semester during which students may add or drop courses without academic penalty |

---

## C

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| CEU | Continuing Education Unit |
| Commencement | The graduation ceremony |
| CIP Code | Classification of Instructional Programs — federal taxonomy for academic programs |

---

## D

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| Degree Audit | A review of a student's academic record to determine if all degree requirements have been met |
| Directory Information | FERPA-designated student information that may be disclosed without consent unless the student opts out |

---

## E

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| Enrollment Verification | Official confirmation of a student's enrollment status, often required by employers or insurance providers |

---

## F

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| FERPA | Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act — federal law protecting the privacy of student education records |
| FAFSA | Free Application for Federal Student Aid |

---

## G

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| GPA | Grade Point Average |
| Graduation Petition | A formal request by a student to waive or substitute a graduation requirement |

---

## I

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| IPEDS | Integrated Postsecondary Education Data System — federal reporting system |
| In-State Residency | Classification determining eligibility for in-state tuition rates |

---

## S

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| SIS | Student Information System — the software platform used to manage student records |
| Standing | A student's academic status (e.g., Good Standing, Academic Probation, Suspension) |

---

## T

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| Transcript | An official record of a student's academic history |
| Transfer Credit | Academic credit earned at another institution and applied toward a degree |

---

## W

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| Withdrawal | The process of dropping all courses after the add/drop period, typically recorded on the transcript |
